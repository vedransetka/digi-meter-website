Project Deliverables · Open Access

Outputs &
Resources

DIGI-METER deliverables will be published here as they are validated and approved for public release. The planned outputs below are indicative; titles and dates will be confirmed against the approved project application.

Indicator Framework & Methodology

Framework Planned

DigComp 2.2 Indicator Mapping Report

A comprehensive mapping of all 21 DigComp 2.2 competences into measurable, context-specific indicators for school and VET assessment. Includes operationalisation guidelines, proficiency level descriptors, and evidence criteria.

[Status TBC]
Framework Planned

Assessment Design Principles & Validation Protocol

Methodological guidelines for assessment item design, bias testing, cultural sensitivity review, and cross-country validation of digital competence indicators across diverse European education systems.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Research Report Planned

State-of-the-Art Review: Digital Competence Assessment in Europe

A systematic literature review of existing digital competence measurement approaches across EU member states, identifying gaps, best practices, and lessons learned to inform the DIGI-METER indicator design.

[Status TBC]

Digital Platform & Technology

Platform Planned

DIGI-METER Self-Assessment Platform (v1.0)

The web-based adaptive self-assessment platform enabling learners to evaluate their digital competences across all DigComp 2.2 areas. Features personalised feedback, progress tracking, and multilingual support.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Platform Planned

Educator Dashboard & Institutional Analytics Module

Class-level and institution-level visualisation dashboards providing educators and school leaders with anonymised aggregate digital competence data, trend analysis, and intervention recommendations.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Technical Report Planned

AI Engine Architecture & Ethics-by-Design Documentation

Full technical documentation of the adaptive assessment AI engine, including model design, bias testing results, GDPR compliance architecture, explainability mechanisms, and responsible AI audit trail.

Planned — date to be confirmed

Piloting & Validation

Research Report Planned

Pilot Testing Methodology & Cohort Design

The research design for multi-country pilot testing, including school and VET partner selection criteria, data collection instruments, ethical research protocols, and comparative analysis framework.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Research Report Planned

Pilot Findings Report: Learner Outcomes & Platform Performance

Analysis of pilot testing results across participating countries: learner self-assessment patterns, competence gap data, platform usability findings, educator engagement, and recommendations for platform refinement.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Open Resource Planned

VET Teacher Digital Competence Toolkit

A practical toolkit for VET teachers to integrate digital competence self-assessment into their regular teaching practice, including lesson guides, discussion frameworks, and CPD activity designs.

Planned — date to be confirmed

Policy Transfer & Dissemination

Policy Brief Planned

Digital Competence Assessment in Schools: Policy Recommendations for EU Member States

Evidence-based policy recommendations for national ministries and EU institutions on embedding DigComp 2.2 assessment tools into national curriculum frameworks and digital education strategies.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Policy Brief Planned

VET Sector Digital Skills Benchmark Framework

A policy-oriented benchmark framework connecting DIGI-METER indicators to VET qualification standards, employer expectations, and national digital skills strategies across participating countries.

Planned — date to be confirmed
Open Resource Planned

Open Educational Resource Pack: Digital Competence for Educators

A freely available collection of learning materials, case studies, and activities supporting educator professional development around digital competence measurement and evidence-based teaching.

Planned — date to be confirmed
