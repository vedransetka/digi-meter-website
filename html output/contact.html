FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

The project will pilot its tools with schools and VET providers across Europe. Participation details and timelines will be announced on this website and in the newsletter once confirmed by the consortium. To express your interest, contact us selecting "Pilot Schools" as your subject.
Access arrangements for the DIGI-METER platform will be announced by the consortium before launch. Details for pilot participants will be shared as part of the pilot onboarding information.
DIGI-METER follows an ethics-by-design approach, with attention to transparency, GDPR compliance, fairness, explainability, and user control. Detailed information about what the platform collects and how it is protected will be published in a dedicated privacy notice before the platform launches.
The list of supported languages is currently being finalised by the consortium and will be announced before the platform launches.
Press and media materials including the project factsheet, visual branding assets, and boilerplate text are available from Outside Media & Knowledge, our communication lead. For interview requests or specific media enquiries, please email digimeter@csicy.com or use the form above with "Media" selected as the subject.
Public DIGI-METER outputs will be published as open-access materials, and the applicable licence will be stated on each resource when it is released. Please credit the DIGI-METER project and European Union funding when citing project materials. For formal research collaboration, contact us via the "Research" subject.
