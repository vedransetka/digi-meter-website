DIGI-METER
Adaptive Platform

An AI-supported self-assessment platform enabling learners to measure their digital competences against the DigComp 2.2 framework — with personalised feedback, educator dashboards, and institutional analytics.

Platform Overview

A Complete Digital Competence Measurement Ecosystem

DIGI-METER is being built as a web-based platform that covers the full digital competence measurement cycle — from individual learner self-assessment through to class-level educator dashboards and institution-level analytics. Every layer is grounded in the DigComp 2.2 framework and designed around privacy-by-design principles.

Ethics & Privacy First

All learner data is pseudonymised. Educators see only aggregated class-level data. Every component is GDPR-compliant and audited by Ergonact's ethics-by-design framework.

Platform Capabilities

What DIGI-METER Does

The platform covers the full digital competence measurement cycle — from individual learner assessment through to institutional-level data and policy-ready reporting.

Learner Experience

Adaptive Self-Assessment

Learners complete an adaptive digital competence assessment that adjusts question difficulty and focus based on their responses. The AI engine maps answers to DigComp 2.2 proficiency levels across all five competence areas.

  • ✓ Adaptive questioning across all 21 DigComp 2.2 competences
  • ✓ Personalised competence profile with proficiency level mapping
  • ✓ Actionable learning recommendations and resource suggestions
  • ✓ Progress tracking across multiple assessment sessions
Educator Tools

Class & Cohort Dashboard

Educators receive an anonymised, real-time view of their class's digital competence profile — identifying where learners excel and where targeted support is needed. All data is privacy-by-design compliant.

  • ✓ Anonymised class-level competence visualisations
  • ✓ Competence gap identification across DigComp 2.2 areas
  • ✓ Exportable reports for institutional reporting and CPD planning
  • ✓ Tracking of learner progress over time
Who Can Use It

Built for Everyone in the Learning Ecosystem

Students & Learners

Understand Your Digital Skills

Take a personalised assessment to see exactly where you are across all 21 DigComp 2.2 competences. Get specific feedback and know what to work on next.

Teachers & Trainers

See Your Class at a Glance

Access class-level dashboards showing aggregate competence data. Identify gaps early and plan targeted learning interventions grounded in real evidence.

Schools & VET Centres

Institutional Reporting & Planning

Use institution-level data to benchmark digital competence across cohorts, track year-on-year progress, and demonstrate impact to stakeholders and inspectorates.

Researchers & Policymakers

Cross-Country Evidence Base

Access anonymised, aggregated research-quality data on digital competence trends across European education systems — informing evidence-based policy development.
